About The Place

Nestled within a vibrant resort just a leisurely 4 minute stroll from the sandy shores, this charming 3-bedrooms, 1,5-bathroom townhouse gestures with its modern allure and beachside ambiance. As you step inside, you're greeted by a spacious open floor plan, where two plush leather sofas and a La-Z-Boy chair invite you to unwind in front of the wall-mounted flatscreen TV. A half bath on the first floor ensures convenience, while the dining area boasts a cozy four-seat round table and three barstools at the open island. Adjacent to the dining space the kitchen equipped with an excess of appliances ensuring every culinary need is met. Arise the elegant staircase to the second floor, where a completely remodeled bathroom and three inviting bedrooms await. Each bedroom with smart TV’s, queen-sized beds with memory foam mattresses, ample storage for your belongings, and individual touches for privacy and relaxation. Outside, two private parking spaces, while a covered patio with a barbecue pit and storage with the beach chairs, beach umbrella, and ice chest provided for your convenience. A washer and dryer stand ready to tackle any sandy laundry, ensuring your beach experience is nothing short of extraordinary. This townhouse located next to the one of the 11 pools and the tennis court in the resort

About The Resort

Nestled just a brief walk from the sun-kissed shores lies a picturesque resort, boasting not only proximity to sandy beaches but also offering a countless of amenities to elevate your stay. With 24-hour security surveillance, peace of mind accompanies every moment of your beachside retreat. 11 pools with 5 of which are heated for year-round enjoyment, while tennis courts and tranquil lakes add to the allure of the resort's recreational offerings. For those seeking the ultimate relaxation, one pool is conveniently situated at the beachfront, granting easy access to refreshment and delectable dining at the adjacent restaurant. For families, the resort's charm extends to its playgrounds, providing endless opportunities for children to play. And for those yearning for a leisurely exploration, the resort offers the privacy of golf carts or bicycles, allowing you to traverse the grounds at your own pace, right next to the sandy beaches and amidst the background of beautiful sunsets. Whether it's a morning stroll along the pristine coastline or an afternoon of fun-filled activities, the resort promises an unforgettable beach experience.
